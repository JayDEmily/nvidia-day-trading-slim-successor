- hide its later reasoning in a way review cannot reconstruct.

## Environmental risk weather versus candidate-specific risk audit

The pack now freezes a crucial split.

### Environmental risk weather

Meaningful before any candidate exists. It is the lane's continuously updated reading of:
- timing and horizon weather;
- event-window weather;
- precursor weather;
- market/sector weather;
- translation-layer weather;
- dependency activation state.

### Candidate-specific risk audit

Activates only when an opportunity-expression candidate exists. It answers questions such as:
- is this candidate acceptable at all;
- should it wait or defer;
- should it be smaller, normal, more assertive, reshaped, hedged, or no-carry;
- which fragility dimensions are most relevant right now.

This split is preserved as a planning obligation without claiming a final runtime packet or a new schema in this gate.

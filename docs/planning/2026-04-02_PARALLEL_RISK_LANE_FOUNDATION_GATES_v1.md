This pack exists to do eight things only:
1. activate a new planning quartet after the closed Gate 156 corrective successor pack and route the repo truthfully to a fresh active gate;

## Why this pack exists

The repo now has no active planning pack routed after Gate 156 closed the stage-local handoff corrective successor tranche. At the same time, exploratory work has clarified one new first-class architecture question and one newly urgent discoverability/lineage problem:
- the repo needs a lawful admission path for an independent parallel risk lane that is first-class but not a numbered `1.1` stage;
- the rich signal/coefficient workbook needs to become discoverable, governed, updateable, and consolidated so later gates do not fork the workbook lineage or break promotion links.

Most surrounding language already exists in repo-native form. The fixed seven-step desk cognition grammar, temporal context, market regime context, options and flow context, calendar horizon routing, event-window semantics, precursor runtime packet, and state-policy coefficient law are all already governed. What is missing is the lawful bridge from that existing language to a new first-class co-resident lane, the workbook consolidation law, and the semantic decomposition needed so the best ideas from the exploratory pass survive into executable leaves.

This pack therefore exists to freeze the missing admission law before any implementation gate tries to write code, mutate packet contracts, or reinterpret the seven-step grammar from memory.
